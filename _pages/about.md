I am a PhD-holding wireless R&D engineer with over 10 years of experience in advanced wireless communication technologies. Currently, I am a **Group Leader** at the NaNoNetworking Center at Catalunya (N3Cat), within the Department of Electronics Engineering at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC).

My research focuses on **ultra-low-power nanoscale systems** for in-body diagnostics and next-generation health monitoring. I am the Principal Investigator (PI) of the **BeNiFIt Project** (€165,313), funded by the European Commission, which aims to reduce diagnostic latency by 1000x through intra-body nanonetworks. 

In 2024, I was awarded the prestigious **Marie Curie Postdoctoral Fellowship** with a score of 97/100. My work has been recognized with the Outstanding PhD Thesis Award, the Qualcomm Paper Innovation Award, and multiple Best Paper awards.

### Research Interests
* **In-body nano-scale networks**
* **Molecular Communications**
* **Bio-inspired ICT systems**
* **AI/ML for wireless communications**
* **Internet of BioNano Things (IoBNT)**
